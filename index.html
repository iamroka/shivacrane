About our Founder

Mr. Sunil Kapoor

Meet Sunil Kapoor, the visionary behind Shiva Crane Service — a trailblazer in the crane industry of Goa. Born and bred in Delhi, a proud Punjabi who made Goa his home over 30 years ago.

With a single crane and a heart full of ambition, he became the pioneer of crane services in the region. Since 1st May 1997, Shiva Crane Service has flourished under his astute leadership, expanding the fleet and services manifold.

Beyond cranes, Sunil Kapoor oversees ventures in iron and steel scrap, hospitality, automobile sales, and heavy machinery parts with a keen eye for opportunity honed over four decades.
